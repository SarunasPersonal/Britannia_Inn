About us

The bars with their range of local real ales are comfortable and cosy. The public bar has a rugby theme with signed shirts from the local rugby clubs including Bath and Bristol and pictures of local teams and sporting heroes. There is a function room on the second floor which is very popular for parties, meetings, auctions and private dinner parties. The Britannia has skittle teams playing every evening in the winter months. The alley is suitable for parties wishing to play skittles, a meeting room or just the place for a party. There is also have a large barbeque facility. This can be hired for the evening or we can provide a complete barbeque for your enjoyment.
